## Abstract

This document proposes an experimental extension to ANP-002 introducing
zero-knowledge proof mechanisms for verifiable autonomous agent behavior.

Building on the Deterministic Stasis architecture (ANP-001), agents can
produce cryptographic proofs demonstrating policy compliance and valid
state transitions without exposing internal data or proprietary models.
